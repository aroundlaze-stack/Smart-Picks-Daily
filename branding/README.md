# Smart Picks Daily — Brand Identity

## Logo Concepts

### Concept 01 — Precision Node ⭐ (Recommended)
A minimal interconnected network/node visual. Blue core with purple and cyan satellite nodes connected by fine lines. Represents smart connections, data-driven decisions, and the tech recommendation ecosystem. Works beautifully at all sizes.

### Concept 02 — Check Shield
A shield with an embedded checkmark. Communicates trustworthiness, verified quality, and protection from bad purchases. The shield conveys safety; the checkmark confirms assurance. Bold, confident, and immediately understandable.

### Concept 03 — Spark Compass
A radar/compass motif with concentric circles and a direction arrow. Represents guidance, discovery, and finding the right path. The purple spark adds a creative intelligence element suggesting AI-powered recommendations.

### Concept 04 — Tech Lens
A magnifying glass combined with circuit/data lines. The lens represents research and discovery; the data lines suggest technology and information. The purple handle accent makes it distinctive and memorable.

### Concept 05 — Minimal S
An architectural geometric 'S' letterform with accent dots. Ultra-minimal, highly scalable, and works as a monogram. The dots add just enough personality without clutter. Strong app icon potential.
